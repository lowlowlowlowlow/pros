What is MxF?

WE'RE GLAD YOU ASKED.

Made by Few was conceived five years ago to inspire designers, developers, creatives, and
entrepreneurs in the startup space. Today, MxF continues to carry that torch and has expanded by
adding additional activities while highlighting the amazing food, people, culture, and talent across
the region.

Basically, it’s the best conference in the South.
